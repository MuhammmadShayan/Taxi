## What is KIRASTAY?

KIRASTAY is your all-in-one solution for hassle-free vehicle rentals in Morocco. We bring together the best local rental agencies onto a single, easy-to-use platform, offering you:

- **Wide Selection**: From compact cars to luxury SUVs
- **Best Prices**: Compare rates across multiple agencies
- **Secure Booking**: Simple reservation process with instant confirmation
- **Multiple Languages**: Available in English, French, German, Spanish, and Arabic
- **Flexible Payment**: Pay online or on arrival with multiple currency options
